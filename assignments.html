Silent Heart Courageuos soul

Silent heart, courageous soul,
A story to be told,
Of a journey through adversity,
Of a journey brave and bold.
In childhood days, a stammering tongue,
A struggle to express,
A habit formed, to keep quiet,
A burden to suppress.
But time marched on, and stammering ceased,
A voice set free to soar,
Yet the habit of silence lingered on,
A shield forevermore.
Now others see a quiet soul,
And deem it cowardice,
But they do not know the strength within,
The courage that persists.
For though the words may not be spoken,
The heart beats strong and true,
And in the face of adversity,
The spirit sees it through.
So let the world see who you are,
And all that you can be,
For a silent heart and a courageous soul,
Is a sight to behold, indeed.
